About EdenBio

EdenBio was founded with a mission to replace chemical-heavy wallcoverings with natural alternatives. We believe that healthier homes and workplaces begin with clean air and safe materials. By bringing the purity of nature indoors, we aim to support both people and the planet.

Core Values

  • Sustainability: Renewable raw materials and eco-friendly production.
  • Health & Safety: Low-emission products for cleaner indoor air.
  • Innovation & Design: Nature-inspired textures for modern lifestyles.

Why Choose Us

  • 100% natural materials, internationally recognized certifications
  • Flexible MOQs and reliable lead times
  • Long-term partnership mindset for global distributors
